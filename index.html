01 / Perspective

I am interested in the architecture of presence—how we build systems that don't just function, but breathe. My work is a search for the logic that lives within sentiment, and the stillness that remains when the noise of the digital world falls away.

Whether through strategy or the written word, I aim to return us to what is already home.
